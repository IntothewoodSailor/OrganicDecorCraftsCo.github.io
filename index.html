About Me

I am an aspiring visual development artist and a highschool student living in the northwestern U.S.

I love to both sketch and digitally draw, mostly in my original style. Art is my current life, but I play guitar and scroll through Reddit in my free time. By the way, I have a habit of doodling on every paper I touch. Recently, I have been working on expanding my commissions more to the public. I hope you enjoy my artworks!
